## Conversion Psychology

### TRADITIONAL APPROACH

**Goal:** Make free feel insufficient

**Tactics:**
- Show what you DON'T have
- Imply you're limited/restricted
- Create urgency (limited time!)
- Use social proof (everyone's upgrading!)
- Feature comparison (look what you're missing!)

**Outcome:**
- ✅ Higher conversion (short-term)
- ❌ Buyer's remorse
- ❌ Trust erosion
- ❌ Higher churn

---

### GUARDRAIL APPROACH

**Goal:** Make plus feel optional but valuable

**Tactics:**
- Show what's locked (not missing)
- Validate free tier ("core functionality identical")
- No urgency (calm decision)
- No social proof (trust your judgment)
- Single plan (no decision fatigue)

**Outcome:**
- ⚠️ Lower conversion (short-term)
- ✅ No buyer's remorse
- ✅ Trust maintained
- ✅ Lower churn
- ✅ Brand strength
